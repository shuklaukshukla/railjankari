Tatkal & Booking Tips

Timings: Tatkal opens at 10:00 AM for AC and 11:00 AM for non-AC trains on the day before travel — be prepared a few minutes earlier.
Multiple devices: Use multiple devices/browsers and keep passenger details ready to speed up booking.
Auto-fill: Use browser auto-fill for passenger names and ID numbers, but verify fields before payment.

Packing & Journey Tips

Carry a small locked bag for valuables and a light blanket for overnight journeys.
Keep digital and printed copies of tickets, ID proofs, and emergency contacts.
For long journeys, pack basic medicines, sanitizer, and a power bank for phone charging.

Food & Cleanliness

Prefer packaged food or order from verified e-catering partners using your PNR.
Carry wet wipes and a small trash bag to keep your berth/seat tidy.

Ticket Cancellation & Refunds

Overview: This section gives general guidance for cancelling tickets and checking refunds. For definitive rules and amounts, always refer to the official IRCTC policy or contact railway enquiry.

E-tickets: E-tickets can usually be cancelled online via the IRCTC website/app until chart preparation. Refunds are processed to the original payment method; timing varies by bank/payment provider.
Counter (PRS) tickets: Cancel at the reservation counter (PRS) before the train departs. Bring the original ticket and ID proof used during booking.
Waitlisted tickets: If a ticket remains waitlisted after charting, it is typically auto-cancelled and refund initiated — manual cancellation is not required for such cases.
Tatkal bookings: Tatkal tickets have strict cancellation rules and are usually non-refundable except in exceptional cases (e.g., train cancellation). Confirm details on official sources before cancelling.
Partial/Group cancellations: You can cancel one or more passengers from a ticket; refunds are calculated only for cancelled passengers after applicable charges.

Quick checklist before cancelling

  • Have your PNR/ticket number handy.
  • Know whether it is an e-ticket or counter ticket.
  • Keep original ID proof ready (for counter refunds).
  • Check whether booking was confirmed, RAC, or waitlisted — refund rules differ.
  • For exact cancellation charges and timelines, consult IRCTC or station enquiry.

Contact & Resources

Official IRCTC website and National Train Enquiry System (NTES) provide live train status and booking. For emergencies call railway helplines available at stations.
